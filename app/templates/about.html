About Bee East Africa Symposium

The Bee East Africa Symposium is the region’s premier platform for promoting sustainable beekeeping, trade innovation, and research collaboration across Africa. Our mission is to empower apiculture stakeholders with knowledge, market access, and technology to transform livelihoods.

Knowledge Exchange

Global experts and regional stakeholders share research, technology, and policy solutions shaping Africa’s apiculture sector.

Market & Trade Opportunities

Connecting beekeepers, agribusinesses, and exporters to investment opportunities and fair markets across Africa.

Sustainability & Innovation

Promoting green technologies and sustainable practices for environmental conservation and community development.

Our Vision

Empowering Africa through Bees

We envision an Africa where beekeeping is not just an agricultural practice, but a powerful tool for sustainability, innovation, and economic empowerment. Bee East Africa serves as a nexus for collaboration among farmers, innovators, and investors to unlock the potential of apiculture.

Through the symposium, we foster cross-sector partnerships to drive research, capacity building, and trade integration in the honey and pollination industry. Our sessions feature technical exhibitions, workshops, and discussions that advance regional collaboration.

  • Regional Impact. Building value chains that connect beekeepers to continental and global markets.
  • Research & Innovation. Bridging the gap between academia, technology, and agribusiness for smarter ecosystems.
  • Sustainability. Advocating for environmental stewardship and climate-smart apiculture.
