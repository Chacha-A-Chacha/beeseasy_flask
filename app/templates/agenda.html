Summit Program

A three-day continental scientific, innovation, and policy summit advancing pollination solutions for Africa

Program Framework

Three Days of Innovation and Action

A structured journey from science and evidence, through innovation and technology, to policy action and investment

Day 1
3 June 2026

Science and Evidence

Examining the state of pollinators in Africa through scientific evidence, exploring drivers of pollinator decline, and understanding the critical links between pollination, food security, and ecosystem resilience.

• Opening session with summit objectives and scientific framing

• State of Pollinators in Africa and Globally

• Pollination, Food Security and Ecosystem Resilience

• Scientific panels on drivers of pollinator decline

• Pollinator ecology in agricultural landscapes

• Pollinator habitats, forests and landscape restoration

Day 2
4 June 2026

Innovation, AI and Technology

Showcasing cutting-edge innovations in AI, robotics, and precision pollination, alongside digital tools for monitoring and management.

• AI, Robotics and Precision Pollination

• Digital Tools for Pollination Monitoring and Management

• Innovation Showcase: Research demonstrations

• Emerging pollination-focused solutions

• Technology exhibitions and product demonstrations

Day 3
5 June 2026

Policy, Investment and Green Enterprises

Advancing policy frameworks, investment pathways, and green pollination enterprises with high-level dialogue on translating science into action.

• Policy & Investment: Enabling Environments for Pollinator Protection

• Green Pollination Enterprises and Climate Positive Markets

• High-Level Policy Dialogue: From Science to Policy Action

• Closing Session: Pathways toward Commitments and Declarations

• Accountability mechanisms and collective action frameworks
