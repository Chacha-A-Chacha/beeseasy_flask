The Importance of Pollinators

Why Bees Matter

Bees are the unsung heroes of East African agriculture. As nature's master pollinators, they play a vital role in increasing crop yields, sustaining ecosystems, and supporting rural livelihoods.

Food Security.
35% of global crop production depends on pollinators, ensuring food availability for millions across East Africa.
Biodiversity.
Bees pollinate 80% of wild plants in our region, maintaining ecological balance and climate resilience.
Livelihoods.
Over 500,000 East African farmers depend on beekeeping for income generation and rural economic development.

BEEASY2025 shines a spotlight on the critical importance of bees to farming, the environment, and economic opportunity in the region and beyond.
