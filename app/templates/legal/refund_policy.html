This policy outlines the terms governing ticket cancellations and refund requests for the {{ event_name }}. Refunds are processed via our payment partner, DPO Group. This policy should be read alongside our full Terms & Conditions.

1. Introduction

The {{ event_name }} (the "Event") is committed to providing a high-quality experience for all delegates. This policy outlines the terms and conditions governing ticket cancellations and refund requests to ensure a transparent process for our attendees and our payment partner, DPO Group.

3. Substitutions and Transfers

  1. If a registered delegate is unable to attend, a substitution may be made at no additional cost. Requests for substitutions must be submitted in writing no later than 7 days before the event.
  2. The request must include the full name and contact details of the original ticket holder and the replacement attendee.
  3. Once a ticket has been transferred, it is no longer eligible for a refund.

How to request a substitution

Email {{ contact_email }} with the subject line "Ticket Transfer Request" and include both the original registrant's details and the replacement attendee's full name, email, and phone number.

4. No-Show Policy

Refunds will not be granted for "no-shows" — delegates who do not attend the event and fail to notify the organizers within the cancellation windows outlined in Section 2.

Failure to attend the event for any reason, including travel delays or personal emergencies, does not entitle the participant to a refund outside of the stated policy.

Important

If you are unable to attend, please consider transferring your ticket to a colleague rather than forfeiting it. Transfer requests must be received at least 7 days before the event.
