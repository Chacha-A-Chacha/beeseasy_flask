Guest of Honour & Keynote Speaker

Rt. Hon. MIZENGO K. P. PINDA

Former Prime Minister of the United Republic of Tanzania

A champion of sustainable agriculture and rural transformation, Hon. Pinda actively promotes pollinator-friendly practices and nature-based livelihoods as tools for income generation and environmental conservation. His strong advocacy has shaped Tanzania's agribusiness landscape and inspired many across the region.

Today, the Pinda Farm in Dodoma stands as a national model for integrated agribusiness and modern beekeeping. Join us as he shares reflections on harnessing pollinator conservation for food security and sustainable development.

Diverse Expertise

What Our Speakers Will Cover

🐝 Pollinator Health & Conservation

Expert insights on pollinator biology, species diversity, disease management, climate adaptation, and habitat restoration strategies tailored for East African contexts—covering bees, butterflies, birds, bats, and more.

🌾 Sustainable Agriculture & Food Security

How diverse pollination services drive crop productivity, nutrition security, and climate-smart agriculture across the region. Learn about pollinator-friendly farming practices.

💼 Market Development & Value Chains

Value chain strengthening, quality standards, export opportunities, and digital marketing for pollinator-dependent products including honey, fruits, and other ecosystem services.

🔬 Innovation & Technology

Modern conservation tools, IoT applications, mobile monitoring systems, citizen science platforms, and breakthrough research transforming pollinator stewardship.

📜 Policy & Institutional Frameworks

Regulatory frameworks, conservation incentives, agricultural subsidies, land-use planning, and cross-border cooperation for pollinator protection.

🌍 Biodiversity & Ecosystem Services

Understanding the interconnected roles of different pollinator species, their contribution to biodiversity, and the economic value of ecosystem services they provide.
