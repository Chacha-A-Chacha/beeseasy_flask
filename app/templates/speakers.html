Guest of Honour

Keynote Speaker

Rt. Hon. MIZENGO K. P. PINDA

Former Prime Minister of the United Republic of Tanzania

A visionary leader and champion of sustainable agriculture and rural transformation. Hon. Pinda has dedicated his career to promoting beekeeping and nature-based livelihoods as powerful tools for income generation and environmental conservation.

National Model: The Pinda Farm in Dodoma stands as Tanzania's national model for integrated agribusiness and modern beekeeping practices.
Policy Influence: His advocacy has significantly shaped Tanzania's agribusiness landscape and inspired countless entrepreneurs.

Keynote Topic: "Harnessing Beekeeping for Food Security and Sustainable Development"
