## PAGE 1: MASTER STATEMENT & SUMMIT OVERVIEW

### 1. MASTER STATEMENT
**Pollination Africa Summit** is a focused, continent-wide scientific, policy and innovation summit with structured programming, technical sessions, and multi-stakeholder engagement, dedicated to advancing pollination as a foundation for food security, biodiversity, livelihoods, and green enterprise development in Africa.

### 2. SUMMIT OVERVIEW
**Placement**: Homepage/About section

**Content**:
Pollination Africa Summit 2026 brings together scientists, policymakers, innovators, development partners, conservation actors, and practitioners to examine the state of pollinators in Africa, explore emerging scientific and technological solutions, and strengthen pathways from evidence to policy, investment, and action.

The Summit is designed as a knowledge-driven, action-oriented platform, advancing scientific understanding, innovation, and cooperative responses to pollinator decline and its implications for agriculture, ecosystems, and livelihoods across Africa.

## PAGE 2: DETAILED PROGRAM STRUCTURE

### PROGRAM HIGHLIGHTS (Preliminary)
**Note**: This replaces the two days program that currently appears on the website

#### Day One: Science and Evidence
- Opening Session: Summit objectives and scientific framing
- Plenary Session: The State of Pollinators in Africa and Globally
- Key Scientific Plenary: Pollination, Food Security and Ecosystem Resilience
- Scientific Panel: Drivers of Pollinator Decline
- Scientific Session: Pollination Science in Agricultural Systems (Productivity, Risk and Resilience)
- Technical Session: Pollinator Ecology in Agricultural Landscapes
- Scientific Session: Pollinator Habitats, Forests and Landscape Restoration
  - *Forests, woodlands, rangelands and restored ecosystems as foundations for pollinator diversity, resilience, and ecosystem services*

#### Day Two: Innovation, Artificial Intelligence and Technology
- Core Innovation Session: AI, Robotics and Precision Pollination
- Applied Technology Session: Digital Tools for Pollination Monitoring and Management
- Innovation Showcase: Research demonstrations and emerging pollination-focused solutions

#### Day Three: Policy, Investment and Green Enterprises
- Policy & Investment Session: Enabling Environments for Pollinator Protection
- Green Pollination Enterprises and Climate Positive Markets
- High-Level Policy Dialogue: From Science to Policy Action
- Closing Session: Pathways toward Commitments, Declarations and Accountability
